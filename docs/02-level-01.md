# 02. Level 01 Details

## Level Name

Level 01 - `The Soup Ladder Incident`

## Level Purpose

This level is the player's first battle and tutorial. It introduces:

- battlefield layout
- tower health and win condition
- troop deployment
- basic economy
- one simple structure slot
- the tone of the world

## Narrative Setup

The level begins moments after the argument at the Festival of Height, Hats, and Soup. Both sides are outraged, confused, and already claiming moral superiority.

The Sunnybottom Empire insists the Mudsnout Horde kicked the sacred soup ladder.

The Mudsnout Horde insists the ladder was "structurally dishonest" and probably fell from shame.

The player is thrown into the first skirmish before anyone can ask useful questions.

## Level Goals

### Player Learning Goals

- understand the main objective
- deploy basic troops
- collect and spend resources
- build one basic structure
- recognize enemy tower pressure
- finish a short battle successfully

### Emotional Goals

- laugh within the first minute
- feel immediately capable
- understand that the world is silly but the gameplay matters

## Match Length Target

3 to 4 minutes

## Visual Theme

The map takes place near the ruined Festival grounds.

Visible details:

- broken banners
- spilled soup cauldrons
- judging signs for tower categories
- decorative ladders knocked over everywhere
- one enormous trophy in the background

## Battlefield Layout

Single lane.

Player side:

- 1 main tower
- 1 rear resource hut slot
- 1 forward defensive slot

Enemy side:

- 1 main tower
- 1 rear resource hut slot
- 1 forward defensive slot

This keeps the level readable and low-pressure.

## Starting Player Loadout

Faction: Sunnybottom Empire

Available troops:

- Spoon Guard
- Pea Shooter Archer

Available build option:

- Snack Shack

Available ability:

- Motivational Yelling

## Enemy Loadout

Faction: Mudsnout Horde

Enemy troops:

- Mud Grunter
- Fork Flinger

Enemy build:

- Slop Bucket Stand

## Tutorial Beats

### Beat 1: Enter the Battlefield

The camera shows both towers and the lane.

Tutorial text:

"That ugly tower across the lane is the enemy. Kindly remove it."

Player action:

- tap to continue

### Beat 2: Deploy a Basic Troop

Prompt:

"Spend Snacks to deploy a Spoon Guard."

Player action:

- deploy 1 Spoon Guard

Lesson:

- units cost resources
- units walk automatically

### Beat 3: Watch the Clash

Two enemy Mud Grunters arrive.

Prompt:

"Units fight on their own. Your job is to decide what arrives and when."

Lesson:

- this is strategy, not direct unit control

### Beat 4: Build Economy

Prompt:

"Build a Snack Shack. Armies run on snacks, bad opinions, and overtime."

Player action:

- build Snack Shack

Lesson:

- economy improves long-term strength

### Beat 5: Defend the Lane

Enemy wave includes Fork Flinger ranged support.

Prompt:

"That one in the back is annoying on purpose. Send another troop."

Lesson:

- ranged enemies matter
- reinforcement timing matters

### Beat 6: Use Ability

Prompt:

"Use Motivational Yelling to buff your troops. It doesn't help emotionally, but it does help numerically."

Lesson:

- simple active ability timing

### Beat 7: Finish the Tower

The enemy tower reaches low health.

Prompt:

"Excellent. Continue diplomacy."

Lesson:

- focus on the final objective

## Win Condition

Destroy the enemy tower.

## Lose Condition

Lose the player tower.

## Level 01 Rewards

- unlock Mudsnout Horde preview in story scenes
- unlock structure slot tutorial complete
- reward: `Golden Soup Ladle`
- reward currency: 100 Coins
- unlock next mission

## Level 01 Enemy Behavior

The AI should be gentle and readable.

Behavior:

- slow opening
- basic troop spam
- one economy structure
- one simple ranged reinforcement wave
- no advanced combos

## Level 01 Dialogue Samples

Emperor Flambard:
"They struck first by being deeply unpleasant."

Chief Grubnuk:
"We struck first by being honest."

Tutorial Guide:
"The tower with your banners is yours. Please try to keep it that way."

## Items Present in Level 01

- Golden Soup Ladle: first collectible trophy
- Snack Crate pickup: temporary bonus resource
- Bent Festival Sign: environmental gag object

## Accessibility Notes

- keep tutorial prompts short
- highlight only one interactive element at a time
- avoid simultaneous popups
- ensure all combat silhouettes are large and clear

## Level 01 Success Criteria

The level succeeds if the player:

- understands the goal without confusion
- uses a troop and a structure
- finishes the level on first or second attempt
- remembers the silly story premise
