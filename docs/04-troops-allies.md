# 04. Troops and Allies

## Purpose

This document defines player-usable units, their battlefield roles, and the tone of troop design.

Troops must be funny to look at and fun to name, but their battlefield job must remain obvious.

## 1. Shared Unit Roles

| Role | Function |
| --- | --- |
| Frontliner | absorbs damage and holds the line |
| Ranged | deals damage from safety |
| Breaker | destroys towers and heavy defenses |
| Support | heals, buffs, or debuffs |
| Siege | converts lane control into tower damage |
| Skirmisher | reaches weak backline targets quickly |

## 2. Faction 1: Sunnybottom Empire

## 2.1 Visual Identity

- polished armor
- shiny helmets
- banners
- spoon and teacup motifs
- serious expressions for completely unserious jobs

## 2.2 Starter Troops

### Spoon Guard

- Role: Frontliner
- Cost: low
- Purpose: tutorial tank unit
- Trait: blocks small amounts of frontal damage
- Personality: deeply proud of being issued a spoon-shaped shield

### Pea Shooter Archer

- Role: Ranged
- Cost: low
- Purpose: early support fire
- Trait: bonus damage against small swarm enemies
- Personality: acts elite despite firing peas at people

### Etiquette Knight

- Role: Breaker
- Cost: medium
- Purpose: anti-structure melee bruiser
- Trait: deals bonus damage to enemy buildings after announcing a formal complaint
- Personality: weaponizes politeness

### Trumpet Medic

- Role: Support
- Cost: medium
- Purpose: heal and morale support
- Trait: healing pulse after a loud brass note
- Personality: believes volume is medicine

### Crumpet Catapult

- Role: Siege
- Cost: high
- Purpose: mid-range tower destruction
- Trait: launches stale pastries with alarming force
- Personality: the crew treats every launch like high art

## 2.3 Advanced Troops

### Certificate Inspector

- Role: Utility Support
- Trait: weakens enemy structures by marking them as non-compliant

### Velvet Ram

- Role: Breaker
- Trait: armored siege ram pushed by suspiciously elegant staff

### Royal Overreactor

- Role: Skirmisher
- Trait: enters combat stronger when allies are damaged

## 3. Faction 2: Mudsnout Horde

## 3.1 Visual Identity

- muddy armor scraps
- bones and rope
- stew stains
- asymmetrical gear
- wildly confident posture

## 3.2 Starter Troops

### Mud Grunter

- Role: Frontliner
- Cost: low
- Purpose: cheap pressure unit
- Trait: gains grit when below half health
- Personality: angry by default

### Fork Flinger

- Role: Ranged
- Cost: low
- Purpose: poke damage from behind frontline
- Trait: chance to briefly reduce enemy move speed
- Personality: throws cutlery like it is a moral argument

### Pot Lid Basher

- Role: Breaker
- Cost: medium
- Purpose: crushes structures and shield units
- Trait: heavy stun on first hit to a building
- Personality: treats every tower like a personal insult

### Gobble Shaman

- Role: Support
- Cost: medium
- Purpose: buffs nearby allies with chaotic stew fumes
- Trait: random minor bonus from a small pool
- Personality: never explains ingredients

### Sludge Lobber

- Role: Siege
- Cost: high
- Purpose: long-range pressure
- Trait: splash slow on impact
- Personality: proud of terrible accuracy

## 3.3 Advanced Troops

### Tunnel Nibbler

- Role: Skirmisher
- Trait: briefly burrows to bypass frontline

### Banner Chewer

- Role: Disruption
- Trait: reduces enemy buffs and morale effects

### Big Auntie Rumblegut

- Role: Heavy Frontliner
- Trait: slow, tanky, and terrifyingly motivational

## 4. Unit Design Rules

- Every unit must have one obvious battlefield purpose.
- Joke naming should not obscure function.
- Units should be legible by silhouette first.
- Starter rosters should cover all baseline needs.
- Advanced rosters should add style, not confusion.

## 5. Upgrade Hooks

Each troop can eventually unlock:

- cost efficiency upgrade
- durability upgrade
- faction-flavored ability upgrade

Example:

### Spoon Guard Upgrades

- Polished Spoon: more armor
- Long Handle: slightly more range on shove attack
- Ceremonial Pride: gains morale faster near towers

### Mud Grunter Upgrades

- Extra Mud: more durability
- Meaner Shouting: higher damage at low health
- Harder Head: reduced stun duration

## 6. Companion and Temporary Ally Concepts

These are not full faction troops, but special mission allies.

### Nervous Referee

- temporary neutral support unit
- blows whistle to interrupt both sides

### Soup Mule

- supply support ally
- slowly delivers Snack bursts

### Angry Architect

- specialist ally in structure-focused missions
- boosts allied building speed

## 7. Troop Count Recommendation

For initial production:

- 5 starter troops per faction
- 3 advanced troops per faction
- 2 temporary ally types

This gives enough variety without overwhelming balancing.
